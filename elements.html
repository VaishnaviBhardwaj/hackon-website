Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moder ate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. There are several measures you can take to protect yourself from #COVID19.
- Wash your hand properly for 20 seconds,
- Do not touch your face,
- Wear mask,
- Avoid Social gatherings,
- Rubbing your hand with an alcohol-based sanitizer,
